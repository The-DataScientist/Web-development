WHAT IS THE JUNIOR ENTREPRISE?

The Junior-Entreprises (J.E.) are non-profit associations with an economic and educational vocation1. Student associations established in higher education establishments,
Junior-Enterprises are defined as a vector of employability and student engagement. By enabling them to put into practice the theoretical education they receive,
Junior-Enterprises constitute a bridge between studies and business, at the service of the professional integration of students.
Thus, they offer services to companies and allow students to put their knowledge into practice by meeting the needs of various clients: large groups, mid-sized companies, SMEs, very small businesses, administration or individuals.
